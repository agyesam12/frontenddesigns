About the Summit

The inaugural Pan African AI Summit in Ghana organized by Alphavecta Technologies aims to be a pioneering event bringing together leaders, innovators, academia, users and enthusiasts from across the globe to explore the transformative potential of artificial intelligence including Generative AI, Predictive AI and Machine Learning.
Connect with global industry leaders to build safe "glocal" AI ecosystem and take your business to the next level. The Pan African AI Summit is uniquely positioned to catalyze the development and deployment of AI-powered solutions that address Africa's most pressing challenges.
